# Download the app

### 📱 For Android/iOS mobile devices
There is no official APK or IPA released yet on Google Play or App Store.

However, you can still have the webapp shown on your homescreen by opening MuxDay in your mobile device and then using the browser settings to find any related to "Add to Homescreen" which shall add a self-contained shortcut to your phone's homescreen for easy access.

### 🛠️ Other OS
Unfortunately we don't natively support other operating systems yet, although we are planning to launch Linux, Android TV, WearOS versions in the future.

> Wanting to hide the fact that you use MuxDay?
> Just use incognito or private tab/window on your browser so that it doesn't save your history and auto-logs you out when you close it.

For any other queries or suggestions, feel free to contact the [developer's account](https://mux8.com/?user=mu) on MuxDay itself.
