SMASH CASTLE RULES

Pre-Match: Everyone moves freely on the grid. Host decides when to START MATCH.

Turns (cycle by join order):

  • First: Choose a tile to HIDE your crown underneath.
  • Then: Strike 1–3 tiles.

A tile breaks after 2 strikes total. If a crown is underneath → it is stolen! Chat announces: “Player X’s crown has been stolen!” (stealer hidden for mystery).

You can have MULTIPLE crowns. On your turn you reposition/hide ONE crown. If you have 0 crowns at the start of your turn, you MUST use all 3 strikes to steal one (RECLAIM MODE). If you still have 0 after your turn → eliminated to audience.

When unbroken tiles ≤ (players × 3 + 1) → everyone teleports to a smaller new map with fresh crowns (1 per survivor).

During anyone’s turn, others are teleported to a tiny 3×3 safe zone (audience view).

Last player standing with crowns WINS!
